6. How the Business Currently Works
Current Workflow:
                  1. A customer or warranty company submits a job

                  2. Job details are received by phone, email, platform, or lead source

                  3. A dispatcher or manager reviews the job

                  4. The job is entered into Housecall Pro or another tracking system

                  5. A technician is assigned manually

                  6. Parts are identified and ordered if needed

                  7. Shipping is tracked manually

                  8. Job status is updated manually

                  9. Follow-up is scheduled if parts are required

                  10. Job is completed and payment is collected or invoiced

Current Operational Friction:
                     * manual dispatching

                     * manual email review

                     * manual technician assignment

                     * manual parts ordering

                     * manual shipment tracking

                     * scattered data across multiple tools

                     * no central AI layer managing tasks

                     * limited automation between software systems

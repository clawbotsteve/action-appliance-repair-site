Frequently Asked Questions

Your appliances are essential to your daily life. We go beyond just fixing the problem — we deliver top-tier service with professionalism and care.

What appliance brands do you service? +
We service all major appliance brands including Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Maytag, Kenmore, Frigidaire, Bosch, KitchenAid, and many more. Our technicians are factory-trained and equipped with parts for the most common repairs.
How quickly can you repair my appliance? +
In most cases, we offer same-day or next-day service. Many repairs are completed in a single visit. For more complex issues that require special parts, we'll order them and schedule a follow-up as quickly as possible.
Do you offer any warranties or guarantees? +
Absolutely. Every repair includes a 90-day parts and labor warranty, and repairs over $500 include an extended 150-day parts and labor warranty. If the same issue comes back during the warranty window, we return and correct it at no additional charge.
What are your service fees? +
Our diagnostic/service call fee is $65, and it is fully waived when you proceed with the repair. We provide upfront pricing before any work begins, with no hidden fees or surprise charges.
Do you service both residential and commercial appliances? +
Yes! We service both residential homes and commercial properties. Our commercial division handles restaurants, offices, laundromats, and other businesses that depend on properly functioning appliances.

Common Sub-Zero Issues We Fix

Compressor Failure

Sub-Zero compressors can fail due to age or electrical issues. We diagnose and replace compressors with genuine Sub-Zero parts to restore proper cooling.

Sealed System Leaks

Refrigerant leaks in the sealed system cause warm temperatures. Our techs are trained in Sub-Zero sealed system repair including leak detection and recharging.

Control Board Problems

Erratic temperatures, error codes, or unresponsive controls often point to a faulty main control board. We repair and replace Sub-Zero electronic controls.

Temperature Fluctuations

Your Sub-Zero not holding temperature? We check condenser coils, evaporator fans, thermistors, and air dampers to restore precise climate control.

Ice Maker Malfunctions

Sub-Zero ice makers can stop producing or dispense hollow ice. We service water inlet valves, ice molds, and harvest mechanisms.

Condenser & Fan Noise

Loud buzzing, clicking, or humming from your Sub-Zero? Often caused by dirty condenser coils or failing fan motors. We clean, repair, and replace as needed.

Common Stove Top Issues We Fix

Stove Top Burner Won't Light

Gas burner clicking but not igniting, or no spark at all. We check igniters, spark modules, gas valves, and burner ports for clogs.

Stove Top Uneven Flames

Flames too high on one side, yellow instead of blue, or flickering. Usually caused by clogged burner caps, damaged orifices, or gas pressure issues.

Stove Top Gas Smell

Persistent gas odor when burners are off is a safety concern. We inspect gas lines, valves, and connections to locate and fix leaks.

Stove Top Electric Coil Not Heating

Coil burner stays cold or heats unevenly. We diagnose faulty heating elements, receptacle blocks, infinite switches, and wiring issues.

Stove Top Knob Issues

Knobs spinning freely, stuck, or not controlling temperature properly. We repair or replace control knobs, valve stems, and switches.

Stove Top Spark Igniter Clicking

Igniter clicks continuously even when burners are off. Typically caused by moisture, a stuck spark module, or a faulty igniter switch.

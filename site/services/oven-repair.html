Common Oven Issues We Fix

Oven Not Heating

Oven won't reach temperature or stays cold. We diagnose heating elements, igniters, temperature sensors, and control boards.

Oven Uneven Temperature

Food burns on one side or cooks unevenly. Common causes include faulty bake/broil elements, broken convection fans, or sensor issues.

Oven Igniter Won't Light

Gas oven clicks but won't ignite, or takes too long to light. We replace worn igniters, gas valves, and safety switches.

Oven Door Won't Close

Oven door doesn't seal properly, letting heat escape. We repair hinges, door springs, gaskets, and latch assemblies.

Oven Self-Clean Not Working

Self-clean cycle won't start or complete. We troubleshoot door lock mechanisms, thermostats, and control board malfunctions.

Oven Error Codes

Display showing error codes or flashing lights. We read and resolve diagnostic codes for all major oven brands and models.
